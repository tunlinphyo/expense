About Expense

The expense was created to make expense tracking easier for myself. It’s built entirely using Web Components for the interface and Firebase for data storage.

You can record your daily spending, organize it by category, and view monthly summaries at a glance.

This is part of my educational practice, so it may not be maintained or supported for long-term use.

If you’re signed in with Google or GitHub, your data is stored securely and managed in accordance with Firebase’s Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.
